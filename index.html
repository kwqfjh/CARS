My Report: Why Electric Cars Aren’t the Future

1. Battery Production Pollution

Electric car batteries require lithium, cobalt, and nickel, mined in ways that damage ecosystems. Mining operations can pollute rivers and destroy habitats.

2. Energy Source Issues

Many electric cars are still charged using coal or gas-powered electricity. The carbon footprint can sometimes match or exceed petrol cars over the vehicle’s lifetime.

3. End-of-Life Waste

Battery disposal is a growing problem. Old lithium-ion batteries are hard to recycle and can leak toxic chemicals.

Benefits of Petrol Cars

Lower production footprint • Global refueling network • Proven longevity • Consistent performance • No reliance on rare-earth mining
